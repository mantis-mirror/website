Will the Mantis Mirror System Fit My Helmet?

Great question—there are many helmet designs out there so we're not going to say we are 100% compatible with all helmets. So, the short answer here is: most likely, yes (as we've looked at many helmets).

Important Note: This version of the Mantis Mirror System is designed for left-side helmet use (for right-hand traffic regions like in North America — including Canada, the U.S., and Mexico).

A right-side version for left-hand traffic regions (e.g., the U.K., Australia) is in development.

The Mantis Mirror System includes a Base Mount plus two adapter wedges (and in rare cases, both wedges can be stacked). As shown in the image above, these options help accommodate various helmet curvatures.

To ensure proper alignment using Mantis’s proprietary marking system, the Base Mount needs to be installed vertically upright on a clean, flat section of your helmet (just above the ear area). The 3M adhesive comes pre-applied for easy installation.

Here’s how it works:

  • Helmet #1 has a near-vertical flat surface—no wedge needed.
  • Helmet #2 curves slightly, so the small wedge is used.
  • Helmet #3 curves even more, requiring the medium wedge.

For helmets with aggressive venting or ridges along the entire sides, ensure that at least the upper portion (1/2" or more) of the Base Mount has direct contact with a flat surface. A wedge can then fill in the lower gap if needed.

A clear installation video is included with your order to walk you through the process step-by-step.
